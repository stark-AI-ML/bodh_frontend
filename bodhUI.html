📡 BodhAPI Documentation

High-performance REST API for Indian news intelligence. Base URL: http://localhost:5000/api

General News

All general news endpoints cover every category unless filtered.

GET/general/v1/today

Today's news ordered by importance.

ParamTypeDefault
limitnumber30
GET /api/general/v1/today?limit=20
GET/general/v1/top

High‑importance news (score ≥ 7) from last 3 days.

ParamTypeDefault
limitnumber30
GET /api/general/v1/top?limit=10
GET/general/v1/crime

Crime news by severity.

ParamTypeRequired
severitystringyes
limitnumberno
GET /api/general/v1/crime?severity=EXTREME&limit=15
GET/general/v1/sentiment

News by sentiment (Positive, Neutral, Negative).

ParamTypeDefault
sentimentstringPositive
limitnumber30
GET /api/general/v1/sentiment?sentiment=Negative&limit=20
GET/general/v1/state

News for a specific Indian state.

ParamTypeRequired
statestringyes
limitnumberno
GET /api/general/v1/state?state=Maharashtra&limit=25
GET/general/v1/entities

News mentioning a person or organization.

ParamTypeRequired
personstringat least one
organizationstringat least one
limitnumberno
GET /api/general/v1/entities?person=Modi&organization=BJP
